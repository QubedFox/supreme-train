About

Welcome to my webpage about encryption and decryption with ciphers.
Here there will be pages on different ciphers and some history behind them. As of now, there is a functional Caesar Cipher with the added
option of using a key to add an extra level of encryption.

Planned additions to the site are an Enigma Machine with history and explanation of how it works (once I figure it out myself)
and other different ciphers used throughout history.
